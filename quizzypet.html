Introduction

A brainstorming quiz to test the intellect of the students about the basics of the oil industry.

Event Description

  • Each team must consist of 2 members only.
  • Questions will be pertaining to the petroleum industry.
  • There will be three rounds
    1. FIRST ROUND: All the teams present will have to answer a set of pre-decided questions on a piece of paper. 10 best teams will be selected from this round.
    2. SECOND ROUND: Visual Round. All the 10 teams will have to answer the questions displayed on the screen. 5 teams will go forward to the next round.
    3. FINAL ROUND: Each team will be asked 25 questions. The questions will have three difficulty levels- easy (10 pts), medium (30 pts), and hard (50 pts). On each turn, the team will have the opportunity to the difficulty level of question it wants to answer.
  • The top two teams will qualify for the 'Mega-Quiz'.
  • Prizes will be given to the top three teams.
  • Certificates and goodies for all participants.
  • Final decision solely rests in the hands of the organisers.
