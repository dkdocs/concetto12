Earth Calling

Introduction

Earth Calling is the paper presentation event, an unparallel event enveloping the wide diversities of mining, geology, geophysics, energy & environmental sciences - a plinth to present your ideas, a platform to show off your skills, a place to learn and grow. Take a small, yet concise topic, present a paper with some inputs from your side and you would do a valuable contribution to the mining industry. We would only be more than eager to welcome you and your brilliant ideas.

Event Structure

This consists of 2 stages: the first one is the online stage which involves the shortlisting of the abstracts submitted while the second one is the presentation of the full paper:

  • First Stage: firstly, participants will have to submit the abstract online. Maximum permissible word limit is 300 words . The abstract should be an accurate representation of your paper. The selection of papers for the second stage will be based on the abstract. If however, the paper presented in the second stage, is found deviating from the abstract submitted, it will be cancelled.
  • Second Stage: The selected papers will be called for the final presentation at the fest. The participant will have to give a PowerPoint/Flash presentation not exceeding 10+2 min. The presentation will be judged by a panel of experts on the following criteria:
    • Originality
    • Technical Depth.
    • Nurture ideas
    • Feasibility/Accountability
    • Presentation Skills.

Topics

IMP: some examples of topics have been given ,But the authors can come up with their own topic but it should be relevant to the given fields.

  1. Mining Advancement: mine planning, mine automation, Exploration techniques, non-conventional mining methods like deep sea mining, gasification, CBM, solution mining, placer mining, mine rehabilitation.
  2. Energy and Environmental Engineering: Topics of Alternative fuels, Air monitoring Equipments, Global Warming, Green Technology, Waste Management, etc.
  3. Geology: Topics on new technologies introduced, mineral sensing, modelling, lunar bodies strata, Lunar mineral identification, Moon minerals, exploration techniques, etc. All presentation rooms will be supplied with a projector for computer presentation, screen, and microphone and laser/stick pointer.

Rules and Regulations

  • The participation in the event may be individual or may be in groups not exceeding 2 members.
  • The abstract submission has to be a word document (not exceeding 300 words).
  • The participants are not allowed to submit any copyright material or any research paper of any Professor etc. If someone is found using any such material he/she will be disqualified from the event.
  • The presentation should not have any embedded audio or visual component.
  • The decision of the panel and organizing team will be final and binding.
  • No student/team is eligible to send more than one entry in the same category; however the same student/team can submit entries in different categories.
  • Top three teams will get certificate of merit. However all other teams would get a certificate of participation.

Eligibility

  • The participant students must be currently pursuing education (undergraduate or postgraduate) in a recognized institute. The participants may be asked to furnish the supporting documents at to prove the fore mentioned criteria.
  • Only teams who have registered online would be able to participate.
