LEVEL 8

Problem Statement

One has to design a fully autonomous bot capable of following a line and detecting obstacles.

Prelims

This is a fastest line follower round. The top 30% of total no. of bots will be qualified for the final round.

ARENA

The track is a simple oval shape track(dimensions will be uploaded soon).

RULES

Penalty=10 pts. for getting out of the track.

Finals

After covering the oval track, the line follower bot has to move thorugh a text [i.e. C 12] shaped path with obstacles (junctions). After that there will be three gates out of which one gate will open which is the end.

ARENA

C12 shape track following three gates.

Rules

  1. For getting out of the track, penalty=10 points.
  2. For restart, penalty=45 points[point of penalties will not be counted for restart].
  3. For failing to detect obstacle, penalty=25 points.
