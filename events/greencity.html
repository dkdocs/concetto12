Green City Modelling

Imagine a socially just, culturally rich and ecologically restorative civilization.

Imagine what tomorrow might hold for humanity if we rise to meet the challenges of today.

We invite the most talented and daring designers, planners, artists and animators to create a new global vision: a breathtaking, compelling city model for the future of civilization. Unleash the power of your imagination to envision a city capable of thriving through the centuries.

Guidelines

  1. The beauty of the city and its sustainability must be visible through the model of the Green City.
  2. The city must display residential, commercial and industrial areas, transportation systems, communication systems etc.
  3. The contents of the city model may vary from model to model depending upon the concept and the ideas involved.

Rules

  1. In the first round participants will be required to draw the theoretical model of the GREEN city which meets the environmental needs of tomorrow in the best possible way on a A-1 size chart.
  2. Tutorials will be provided soon based on which you can build your model.
  3. Demonstrate the green city infrastructure for overall ecological balance.
  4. Innovative and technical ideas of the participants regarding the conservation of the natural resources applicable to the society will be highly appreciated.
  5. Briefly describe the idea of the project in the form of an abstract (word limit: 250 words).
  6. The selected entries have to present their models for which instructions would be given later.

Crucual Points

How will your project benefit the community? How sustainable is your concept or ideas? Why do you think your project is the cheapest solution to the environmental problems of today?
