Introduction

This is a management event testing your ability to analyse completely sudden and strategic situations that often come across the way of entrepreneurs. Being a part of this magnanimous event gives you a chance to feel the difference between thinking and actually being an entrepreneur. It will make you feel puzzled and stressed but in the meanwhile it will be teaching you the pros and cons of putting a start-up.

It is an on-the spot event selecting the BUSINESS BAAZIGAR in three rounds. Shortlisting will be done in the first round which is a general quiz regarding the Ad-World. The details of the other two rounds will be disclosed on the spot.

So, put all your mettle, compete with others and become the BUSINESS BAAZIGAR of the event.

Winners will receive exciting cash prizes!

Rules

  • Maximum team size will be of 2 members.
  • Any number of teams may participate in the first round but only 8 teams out of those will be shortlisted for the 2nd round.
  • Four teams from the second round will be promoted to the finals.
  • The decision of the judging panel shall be binding in case of any conflict.
  • In case any participant uses fraudulent means to clear the first round shall be debarred from any further participation.
