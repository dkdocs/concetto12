Are you crazy about hacking? Want to learn advanced ethical hacking?

This year Indian School of Mines organizes "HackTrack 2012"-Three days Workshop on Ethical Hacking and Information Security in association with TechDefence Pvt. Ltd.
The workshop is specially designed by TechDefence in co-ordination with CONCETTO 2012, Indian School of Mines for aspiring hacking and information security geeks.
