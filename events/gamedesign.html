Game Designing and Scripting

What do you Learn?

Fascinated by NFS? Create your own tracks and learn how to make racing games in 3D. An innovative and wonderful course curriculum designed by our research team, will teach all the basics of game designing. The game designing market still being in the nascent stage this workshop surely will add wings to those who want a career in this domain. It is surely going to open a new horizon in terms of career opportunities.

  • Introduction to Scripting
