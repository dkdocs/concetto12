You think you are tech savvy... Do your nerves tickle as you gain any technical information..????
If it is so, here comes a platform to prove it.

Tech Quizzard comes up with 2 extremely difficult quizzing rounds, with questions covering all the sectors ranging
from recent advances in technologies, famous personalities in this sector to their role in nation's economy and
making our world a global village.

But just knowledge about technology won't be the only thing you'll need. We make sure that your decision making
capabilities, also come in play.

Mind you when I say these questions will test your quizzing skills to the deepest level and extract the quizzarding
genius out of you..


     RULES

There will be two rounds (QUALIFYING ROUND and STAGE ROUND).

Qualifing Round:

  1. A team must comprise of 2 members.
  2. Each team will be provided a set of 45 questions divided into 3 genres. 30 minutes will be allotted
    to answer the questions.
  3. Phonetically correct answers will be accepted - this is a test of your knowledge NOT spelling.
  4. Best 2 genre scores are aggregated to determine the score of each team. Your lowest score will
    matter only in the event of tie.
  5. In the event of a tie, the score you dropped from your lowest scoring genre will be taken into
    consideration. If there is still a tie, the person with the highest scoring paper in any genre will win. If
    there is still a tie the player whose next best score (from any category) is highest (and so on) will be
    deemed the winner.
  6. Top 5 scoring teams qualify for the next round.
  7. In case of any dispute, the decision of the EVENT ORGANISERS will be final.

Stage Round:

  1. This will consist of FOUR rounds.
  2. First round will be direct Question Answer round.
  3. Remaining three rounds will be based on the AUCTION system, for which each team will be given
    100 Bid points (only once) at the starting of the STAGE round. Rules for which are as follows:
    • At the beginning of each round 5 objects will be introduced to the 5 teams.
    • Objects could be image, audio clip, video clip, movie posters etc. However all the objects of a
      round will be of same kind, e.g., second round may consist of 5 images to be auctioned among the
      teams.
    • At the end of every round the BID points for each team will be revised as:
          New Bid Points= Old Bid Points-Bid placed in the round
    • Minimum Bid allowed to be placed by any team will be 1 Bid point to fetch an object.
    • Any team cannot place bid above point score held by it (100 Bid points in second round and New
      Bid Point       in subsequent round).
    • In each round a set of Questions will be asked in relation to the object won by each team. By
      answering these questions team will earn GAME points.
  4. Maximum Game point a team could earn in a round are as follows:

         Round I: 300 points

         Round II: 300 points

         Round III: 400 points

         Round IV: 600 points

  5. In case of tie, team earning maximum GAME points in round IV will be declared winner. If still a tie, round III
    scores will be taken into consideration, and so on. If still there is a tie, rank of the team in the qualifying round will
    decide. Remaining BID points will not influence the final rankings.
  6. In case of any dispute, the decision of event organiser will be final.
