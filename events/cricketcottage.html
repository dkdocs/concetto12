Cricket Cottage

Introduction

Have you ever wondered what it is like to be one of the team owners in IPL auction? Concetto 2k12 brings you the opportunity to show your bidding skills. The game of cricket is old and so is its spirit. And for the wandering souls of cricket is the mathematical modelling based Cricket Cottage. So backpack your own strategy, stats and analysis and be ready for the battle of wits.

Rules and Regulations

  • A team shall consist of maximum three members
  • Questions on players included for bidding and their prices will not be entertained
  • In case of any dispute, the decision of the organising team will be final
  • The algorithms and mathematical model used for the decision of winning teams will not be changed under any circumstances
  • Use of any unfair means-mobiles, discussions, internet etc., during the elimination rounds and bidding phases will lead to immediate disqualification

Winning prices will be announced during conduction of the event.
