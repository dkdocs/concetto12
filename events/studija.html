Studija Primera

Introduction

Presenting a case requires utmost communicational skills and understanding of technical and economical aspects. In this competition participants will be required to examine and analyze the problem and produce a feasible solution for the same.

Event Description

Priliminary Round

A real life problem or development aspect of a enterprise or industry will be provided by the enterprise/company itself. Participants have to present a case study with a viable solution on the given problem statement.

Problem statement will be uploaded soon.

An abstract for the Case study should be submitted in Microsoft word (.docs) format which would represent your idea. The abstract must not exceed 200 words and the participants are required to send their case studies to any of the email addresses given in contacts in the end, on or before 25/09/2012.

List of shortlisted teams for main event will be displayed on the fest website on 28/09/2012.

Main Event

Selected teams have to present their solution in power point presentation with maximum 10 slides within 7 minutes and 3 minutes would be given for questioning round for judges and audience.

Rules

  1. Participants are required to participate in teams consisting 2-4 members.
  2. Participants from different institutions can form a team.
  3. All participants must bring their original identity card provided by their institute.
  4. The result declared by the judges will be binding and final.
