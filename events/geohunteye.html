Geohunt Eye

Here is an opportunity for all to fulfill his desire to find out the treasure. Concetto ,2012 bring an opportunity in the form of 'Geohunt Eye', a treasure hunt event to test your awareness and geological knowledge of your planet.

Event Structure

  • Each team will have to search for a 'TREASURE' following a particular path leading to the treasure. Hints and clues at different spots will be hidden within the arena which will help the team in searching out the next hints or treasure.
  • Some bonus points are also available within the arena.
  • Points will be awarded to the team according to their success in finding hints/solving puzzle at each stage.

Rules

  • Each team will consist maximum of 3 members. There is no constraint on kind of team formation.
  • The team completing the task first will be declared as winner.
  • All hints, puzzles, pictures, specimens which will be used in the quiz is only from earth science domain ( Geology, Geophysics, Petroleum Engg.,Mining engg., Mineral engg. Environmental engg.Etc.)
  • Time allotted to complete the task will be 90 minutes.
  • Any team trying to leave the decided arena, to talk to any officials of event, to damage or waste any hint or materials available will be disqualified immediately.
  • If any team select any one of the hints available, points will be deducted for selecting the another hint at same stage as 20, 30, 40 for touching 2nd, 3rd and 4th hint respectively.
  • Ultimate task is to find the treasure and your 'Geohunt Eye' will lead you to the treasure.
