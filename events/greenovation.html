Greenovation

For decades we have been ignoring Mother Nature in pursuit for our own selfish needs, but now we are at the precipice and so we have to change our way of doing things in order to ascertain our continued existence. The need of the hour is to adopt sustainability, both in order to solve the rising energy crisis and pollution and for this awareness is required. Before taking any kind of action planning is required and thus we invite the most intelligent minds to create a way for our sustainable future.

The event will be organised in two independent rounds:

Case Study Round

In this round students will have to submit a solution on the given problem statement, and depending on the innovation in their ideas and the feasibility of solution teams would be qualified for the next round.

Presentation Round

In this round the students will be asked to suggest some methods on how to bring awareness among the general public on the generation of energy using renewable sources. They will also have to suggest ideas to convince the people for efficient use of these energy sources. Marks will be given in this stage on the basis of the uniqueness of their ideas.

Final Result

The marks obtained in these two rounds will be added up to give the final result. The best ideas may be sent to JREDA and some science periodicals if found exceptional.

  4. The case study for the first round must be sent to raunakjain0991@gmail.com before 20/09/2012. Late submissions will not be accepted. It must be in 4-6 pages (A4) with:
