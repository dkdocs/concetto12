Tech Spade

Introduction

Did you know: Newton never formulated the equation of his second law of motion? Do you drool over the latest gadgets? If you are confident with you tech trivia, then this is your chance. This quiz is going to test your knowledge for technology and above all your passion for quizzing itself. We invite you all for this thrilling session, watch while the teams battle it out on stage as questions and answers fly back and forth. Sounds interesting, doesn't it? With some of the best technical brains vying for top honours, you cannot afford to miss this event. Even if you are a beginner for quizzing, do turn up!! You never know the very thing you read somewhere, may win you goodies and other exciting gifts.

Rules and Regulations

  • The quiz will be held in 2 rounds- preliminary and finals.
  • The preliminary will be a written elimination round.
  • The top 6 teams from the prelims qualify for the finals.
  • The decision of the Quiz Master will be final and binding.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Entry is restricted to students currently pursuing education in a recognised institute.
  • Students from different colleges can form a team.
  • Each team can contain maximum of 3 participants
