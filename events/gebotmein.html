Introduction

Seeing the declining trend of interest of students in mining field, Students of Indian School of mines, Dhanbad present to you a platform to connect the fresh mining engineers to the Industry giants. This Panel would consist of six or eight persons (will be CMD/ GM of the firms of the Mining sector/ Enterprise or various dignitaries of SAIL, CIL, TATA STEEL, NMDC, and DGMS). The first phase shall provide a platform where students can present their queries in front of the Panel and in the second phase, a problem statement will be provided to students, to test their mine planning and designing skills and cash prizes will be provided to the winners.

Phase 1

First of all, the Panelists/audiences will be indicated to the objective of the panel discussions which will give them a brief idea, prior to the discussion. The panelist will then discuss the issue or topic by asking the questions or reacting to the views and opinions of the participants and then raising their queries to the Panel. The Questions or the Queries could range from a wide variety of technically drafted issues to personal queries about mining industry and its prospects.

Phase 2

Stage One

The first Stage would be an Online abstract and Flow plan submission by the teams on the basis of the Problem statement and the Mine Plan. The Top Ten Abstracts would be selected and each team would be provided with a Virtual Multi National firm that suits in with the structure of the event. The role players would then have to fit in with the pros and cons to the virtual situation to gain the most out of their present technological aspects (Present technology of the Firm), the format of their tender representation and their way of operation.

Stage Two

Second stage would be a General body Discussion/ Round Table Conference to test and hone the skill of the Teams involved on the basis extensive discussion with the governing body /Judge panel. The team would be required explain their mine plans and project Ideas amongst themselves and the Panel, followed by the filling of tender and their biding amount in the prescribed formats of their allotted firms. The best five teams selected after second stage would then be eligible for final panorama.

Final Stage

The Last stage would be Personal Discussion/ Interview of the Teams with the Judge panel testing the feasibility of their plans and projects through a cut throat and diplomatic assessment session with the panel.

Eligibility

  • The participant students must be currently pursuing education (undergraduate or postgraduate) in a recognized institute. The participants may be asked to furnish the supporting documents at to prove the fore mentioned criteria.
  • Only teams who have registered online would be able to participate.

Rules and Regulations

  • The participation in the event must be in groups not exceeding 3 members representing the characters and decision makers of this firm, irrespective of dept. and college.
  • The abstract submission has to be a word document (not exceeding 500 words) with a outline of the Flow Plan to the given problem statement.
  • The participants are not allowed to submit any copyright material or any research paper of any Professor etc. If someone is found using any such material he/she will be disqualified from the event.
  • The decision of the panel and organizing team will be final and binding.
  • No student/team is eligible to send more than one entry in the same category with the students representing
  • The Teams would be judged on the basis of the cumulative score of the three rounds and the following criteria.
    • Economics
    • Plan feasibility
    • Presentation Skills
    • Question Round
  • Top three teams will get certificate of merit. However all other teams would get a certificate of participation.
