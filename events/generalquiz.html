General Quiz

Introduction

Quizzing was once considered limited only to General Knowledge Questions, but with time it has evolved into a Mind Sports and the Quizzers are seen as intellectual persons pursuing a serious Hobby. With Questions from all topics and genere possible on earth and space, its only easy if you either know the answer or can crack it using given hints. So if you are one of those peoples who like to live hungry and foolish for knowledge, facts and fundae, then its your heaven.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Maximum Team size 3; Minimum size 1.
  2. The quiz will be held in 2 rounds- preliminary and finals.
  3. The preliminary will be a written elimination round.
  4. The top 6 teams from the prelims will qualify for the final round.
  5. Entry is open only for College Students.
  6. Students from different colleges can form a team.
  7. The decision of the Quiz Master will be final and binding.
