Catalyca

Paper-de-chemical engineering invites all those fresh though innovative minds to present their research or review paper and become a part of what could help to bring your research and career reach new heights.

Rules and regulations

  • Each team may consist of 2-3 members.
  • Invited entries from every branch will be entertained.
  • Teams have to send their abstracts (word limit 500 words, font Times New Roman, font size 12) possibly up to 20th September 2012 to catalyca2012@gmail.com.
  • Selected teams will be notified through the E-mail and regular updates on the Website by 25 th September 2012.
  • The selected teams will have to present their papers during 4-6 October in Concetto 2K12.
  • The event will primarily focus on the following subjects
    • Future Fuels and feasibility on macro levels
    • Bio-degradable polymers
    • Latest advancements in petroleum refining
    • Environmental friendly fertilizers

(The above topics presented here is a major concern to the invited companies).
The time limit for presentation is 8 minutes+ 2 minutes for question (optional).

Judges

The judge panel would include important personalities from chemical and petroleum industries like Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. and Honorable HOD of Chemical Engineering, Prof. V. K. Saxena. The Decision of the Judges would be final and binding.

  • Winning certificates will be provided to the first, second and third teams in proper hierarchy.
  • Besides this, certificates to the participating teams will also be provided by the renowned companies of the Industrial sector.
