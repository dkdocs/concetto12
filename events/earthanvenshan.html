Earthanvenshan

Introduction

Earthanveshan is basically an earthscience quiz covering current geoscience affairs.it is to check the aptitude of a participant about geographical and geological facts. No pre-requisite knowledge reqiured to participate in this quiz.

Rules and Regulations

  • A team consist of maximum of 2 members.
  • The quiz will consist of 3 round.first round will be qualification round followed by two knockout rounds

Event Description

Round 1 (visual round)

A documentary featuring origin and development of any geological feature will be shown followed by question and answer round related to the documentary Example-video showing origin of himalayas *12 teams would be selected from this round

Round 2

Consists of 15-20 questions about geological facts. Image related questions , geogaphical/geological phenomenon will be arranged in order Example-arranging mountain peaks according peaks according to their heights from the sea level or geological phenomenon as per time map. Example2-picture of amazon river shown and facts asked on it *5 teams would be selected to the final round

Round 3(final round)

A rapid fire round of current geo-science affairs.time limit 1 minute per team Followed by a map tagging round Participants will have to locate some places and topographical features in the maps provided. Example-locating the largest lagoon in india
