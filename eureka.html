Introduction

Ever wondered why kids who play video games have better reflexes than those who don't? Why people who use internet explorer are crazier than those who use mozilla? The time has come for the most innovative minds to present their pathbreaking ideas before a panel of expert judges.

Rules

  1. Participants are required to participate in teams consisting of not more than 3 members.
  2. Participants from different institutions can form a team.
  3. All participants must bring their original identity card provided by their institute.
  4. The result declared by the concept team will be binding and final.
  5. The abstract has to be submitted to the undersigned in a word document (not exceeding 500 words).
  6. Best abstracts will be selected for the paper presentation, in the final round of the competition.
