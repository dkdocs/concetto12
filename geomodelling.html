Geo Modelling

Introduction

Geo-Modelling is basically a depiction of the inherent processes taking place in the Earth surface as well as in the sub surface, mainly through static also with some dynamic models. It also essentially includes the processes and the means of various geological activities like seismic data acquisition, exploration & exploitation, process and mechanisms of the sub-surface methods etc. Generally it gives a broader perspective and the different aspects of their geological applications. Prize worth INR 10,000 to be won!

Problem Statement

Participating team need to make and present a static or dynamic model. Model should be innovative and should present a theme based design. Seismic data acquisition, exploration & exploitation, process and mechanisms of the sub-surface methods etc. can be selected as theme; or other theme can also be taken.

Rules and Eligibility

Following are the criteria for the participation in the Geo-modelling contest.

  1. No. of participants in a team must not be more than 3 members
  2. Participants must be students of an undergraduate/ Integrated or a post- Following graduate course.
  3. The prepared model should be of a geological setting or a geological phenomenon and the same must be explained by the participants at the time of the competition.
