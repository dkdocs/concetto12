MINERIA ENCUESTA

Introduction

MINERIA ENCUESTA brings to you with an opportunity to test your surveying skills with confined resources at your hand. This event will check your ability of working in teams to extemporize in situations of crisis to complete the given task. Come and show your innovative, creative and technical skills and your ability to handle pressure while competing against time and opponents to complete the most important tasks of surveying.

Prize worth INR 15,000 to be won !!!!

Event Structure

  • Phase 1: It's an elimination round where all the teams will be tested for basic surveying tasks and quizzes (based on surveying). Based on the points the teams earn, the top 10 teams will be qualified for the Phase 2 of the event.

  • Phase 2: Teams qualified in Phase 1 will have to draw a card out of 10 cards randomly on which task to be done will be written. With the help of basic surveying instruments (such as auto level, tilting level, etc.) the team will have to perform the given task with in a prescribed period.

  • Phase 3: This is a challenging phase in which the teams will be given some surveying instruments. These instrument may range from as complex as theodolite to as basic as magnetic compass . With help of these instruments, teams will be required to complete the given survey task (details will be given in Problem Statement on the spot).

Rules and Regulations

  • The number of students in a team should not be more than 3.
  • Only Top three teams will get certificate of merit. However other teams would get a certificate of participation.
  • The teams interested in Participation can register on the website as well as on the spot.
  • Decision of Judges will be final and binding.
  • The point system and other details will be provided on the spot.
