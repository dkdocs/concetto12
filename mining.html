Event Structure

  • The event will be conducted in 2 phases:
    • 1st Phase: - Online abstract submission.
    • 2nd Phase: - Will be held at ISM Dhanbad during CONCETTO'12.
  • Abstract must consist of:
    • Approach to problem.
    • Process: How to proceed
    • Economy: Cost evaluated.
    • Political: Land Evaluation, democratic value.
    • Environmental Consideration
  • Short listing will be done on the basis of abstract evaluation and short listed participants will be invited to ISM for 2nd phase.
  • 2nd phase comprises of presentation (detailed description of the process used, cost evaluation and politics involved) followed by a question-answer round in the presence of other participants and judges (a well-known person of industry).
  • The winner will be declared on the cumulative evaluation of 1st and 2nd phase.

Rules

  • Each team can contain a maximum of 3 participants. Students from different colleges can form a single team.
  • Teams should mention their college name and the names of their members on the first page of the abstract.
  • The abstract should not be longer than 250 words.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and binding.
  • Only Top three teams will get certificate of merit and handsome amount as appreciation. However other teams would get a certificate of participation

Eligibility

  • The participant students must be currently pursuing education (undergraduate or postgraduate) in a recognized institute. The participants may be asked to furnish the supporting documents at to prove the fore mentioned criteria.
  • Only teams who have registered online would be able to participate.
