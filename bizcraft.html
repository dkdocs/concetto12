Bizkraft

You were born to win, but to be a winner, you must plan to win, prepare to win, and expect to win. – Zig Ziglar

Change is always difficult. Entrepreneurship is often the most difficult but also the most courageous and risk taking decision in an individual's life. An efficient and practical Business plan leads an Entrepreneur to the path of success. Genesis E-Cell, ISM Dhanbad in association with Nurture Talent Academy (www.nurturetalent.com) proudly presents Bizkraft 2012, an annual business plan competition of Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad.

If you dare to take risk and inspire to become entrepreneur we have the opportunity that you have been waiting for! Put together a top-notch business plan and bring it to the Bizkraft. Bizkraft, one of the most important activities on campus in the annual techno-management fest- "Concetto", is the business plan competition, which is a mezzanine level. Genesis, E-cell ISM Dhanbad is devoted to helping promote, support and sustain entrepreneurship in the young aspiring minds.

Participants stand to benefit and gain in following manner

  1. Great Networking opportunity with Investors, Mentors, Incubators and other Ecosystem players.
  2. Direct entry into the MSME(Micro Small and Medium Enterprise) and chance to get Incubation money of INR 6.5 lakhs from the side of MSME for the top three entries and a recommendation from our side too.
  3. Exciting Cash Prizes to be given to the top three winners.
  4. Development through strong backing and guidance by our associate organizations.
  5. To provide a meaningful learning experience for participants who are interested in pursuing an entrepreneurial opportunity at some point in their careers.
  6. Webinars to increase your understanding of how to 'Make it Large' while starting a venture

Rules and Regulations

Eligibility criteria and team composition

Eligibility

  • Students, Start-ups and Working Professionals from all over the world can participate
  • Entries must be the original work of entrants.
  • Individual participation is allowed in the Competition though participation in teams is encouraged.
  • The plan should solve a critical social problem in an innovative, transformational way.
  • The mission and practices of the venture must focus on quantifiable social return.
  • The venture must be for-profit and financially viable; non-profit entries will not be accepted.

Team Size: 4

  • The event will be held in three stages.
  • the maximum team size for all the rounds will be 4.
  • On the basis of the performance in the first round, 10 teams will be selected for the final round.
  • Entries submitted after the deadlines will not be considered.
  • The judges, mentors and the organizers will guarantee the utmost confidence of the entry or idea.

Stage 1

  • Executive summary of the plan has to be submitted.
  • The submission will be online.
  • Rules and restrictions on the executive summary:
  • The executive summary shall be submitted in a pdf or doc format only. No other form of submission will be accepted.
  • The last date of submission of executive summary for the 1st round of this event is 25th September,2012.
  • Short listing of teams will be done on the basis of their executive summaries and innovation.
  • 10 Teams to be short listed after Round - I.

Stage 2

  • Mentoring Period - 27th September to 3rd October,2012.
  • Separate E-mentors would be provided to all the 10 finalists.
  • Adequate mentorship to all the 10 entries during this period from our associate Nurture Talent Academy.
  • Mentors for each team would be chosen by us

Stage 3

Final Presentation

  • The 10 teams will give their presentation in front of the judges.
  • The teams will have to give a presentation. Each team will get 12 minutes to present their B-plan. They will have 3 minutes to wind up.
  • After the presentation there will be a 5 minute question answer session by the judges.
  • 10% marks will be deducted for every minute exceeded after 15 minutes.
  • Participants will have to give their presentation in Macromedia flash 8.0/Microsoft Power point.
  • Picture clippings and graphs may be used.
  • Recorded dialogues or movie clippings are not allowed.
  • Laptops for presentation will be provided by us.
  • Entries remain the property of the legal owner.

The contestants must agree with the rules of the contest and must agree to accept the declaration of confidentiality.

Submission

Executive Summaries

  • Executive Summary Submission ends on: 25rd September,2012.
  • Announcement of Stage 1 results on: 27th September,2012.
  • Final Presentation on: 4th October onwards
  • Executive summary should be in PDF or DOC format.
  • Format of Executive Summary: Times New Roman, Size '11', A4 page.
  • Name your file as BIZKRAFT_'Team Name' and mail it to genesis@ismu.ac.in .
  • The executive summary should be of maximum three to five pages excluding the cover page.
  • Cover page should contain: * Title of the Business Plan
    * Institute Name
    * Name of the team & Team ID
    * Full Names of all Team members
    * Name of the nominated team leader
    * Contact Information (mail id, mobile no) of all team members.

B-Plans

  • All the ten finalists had to submit their final B-plan by 2nd October,2012.
  • B Plan should be in PDF or Power point presentation format.
  • Format details :Times New Roman, Size '11', A‐4 page.
  • File name should be: BIZKRAFT_'Name of Your Team' Final and mail it to genesis@ismu.ac.in by October 2nd, 2012, 12:00 noon deadline.
  • B Plan should not exceed 20 pages/slides.

Judging Criteria

CATEGORY DESCRIPTION DESIRED
Business Concept/Opportunity Identify loopholes, problems in the system. Find out the narrow line you would walk on Generates interest, fascinating, high growth and realistic
Product or Service Describe the product or service Complete description(eg. Prototype explanation followed by list of approving agencies)
Competetive Advantage Analysis of existing competitors strengths and weaknesses, description of why your product is better Patented product or service, first to enter the market, lower cost structure
Market Size How big is the size of the market? What are the growth prospects? Entry and Exit barriers Large or rapidly expanding market suited for high expected profits
Customer Value Is the product a necessity or a luxury? What are the benefits to the customer? High customer value, possibly supported by market survey, list of first customers with feedback if any
Customer Segment Who is the target customer? Requirements of the customer and how your product addresses those demands. Clearly defined target group-geographic expanse, income group etc., clear understanding of the customer needs and expected response
Revenue Model How will profit be generated? Marketing and Sales Channels. Short term goals Justified projections and short term goals. Vivid plans for marketing and sales.
Finance Analysis What is the initial investment required? Expenditure projections for 1-3 years. Justified investment value. Clear planning of expenses.
Team Detailed account of the team members Team covers all major areas , experienced team members with complementing skill sets
Business Plan on the whole On the whole ,highly attractive and persuasive in all aspects. A business concept with great potential, clear prospects for success, and an inspiring presentation

NOTE: The decision of the organizers and the judges is final and binding.
