MATRIXVILLE

Introduction

Matrixville validates your abilities and skills of basic MATLAB computation, programming and simulation. This is for all engineering enthusiasts who are curious about solving practical problems with easy computational and simulation based platform. As nowadays these types of approaches are very common to all engineering disciplines, so build yourself able to pace with the modern world.

Rules And Regulations

  1. The team will consist of 2 members.
  2. A participant can belong to a single team only.
  3. The problem statement for both rounds will be given on the spot.
  4. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  5. After submission of solutions no corrections will be entertained thereafter.

Rounds

  1. Qualifying round will be based on matlab basics and coding based on matlab environment. After qualifying round 8 teams will be shortlisted for final round.
  2. Final round will check your simulation knowledge and have to be solved by using Matlab at the spot using simulink tool. The simulation must run completely after your submission. The Judgment will be based on logic and simplicity of the algorithm used.
