Introduction

Pure mathematics is the world's best game. It is more absorbing than chess, more of a gamble than poker, and lasts longer than Monopoly. It's free. It can be played anywhere - Archimedes did it in a bathtub. ~Richard J. Trudeau, Dots and Lines

For the first time Concetto brings online maths quiz and it's not going to be just numbers and puzzles but something that lets you discover the underlying beauty of 'the' MATHEMATHICS. This quiz tries to pursue the most beautiful tradition of mathematics, by teasing you with something very obvious but finally making you think about it for hours in the end.

You are required not just to find answers but provide a proof because Mathematics without proof is same as Indian cricket team without Sachin. So get ready to challenge your grey cells in this mysterious world of unrelenting challenges. We will try to minimize the domain of the questions as far as possible.

Sample Questions at:- http://tough-nut.blogspot.in/

Rules and Regulations

  • Only Individual participation is allowed. Cooperation between the two or more people is strongly discouraged and will result in disqualification.
  • Participant can hail from any school or college.
  • Event will start in September and Problem Set will appear on a weekly basis.
  • The participant with maximum marks after all the problem sets, will be declared the winner.
  • Our Focus is on the ideas and the thought process rather than the answers itself. So, make sure there is no room for any vagueness in your proof.
  • In case of a tie, the participant scoring more marks on some special question appearing in each of the problem set.
  • You have to submit your answer in the LATEX writing or by scanning your solutions in the pdf format only. (Make sure your solution is legible in case of scanning).
