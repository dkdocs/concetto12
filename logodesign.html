Logo Design Competition

Introduction

A logo is a graphic mark or emblem commonly used by commercial enterprises, organizations and even individuals to aid and promote instant public recognition. Logos are either purely graphic (symbols/icons) or are composed of the name of the organization (a logotype or wordmark). Design a logo on any topic and submit here with your theme. You can also modify and make your own logo with your creativity of any existing organization or company.

Rules

  • Logos have to designed in CorelDraw or Adobe Illustrator
  • Logos have to be a vector graphic i.e. on zooming the logo should not pixelate.
  • A complete description of the logo is required when submitting.
  • A copy of Source file is to be submitted.
  • Any discrepancy found by the coordinators will lead to disqualification.
  • Tag-line is to be attached with each logo.

Scoring

Scoring will be based on three criteria

Design of your logo 40 credit
Message depicted 50 credit
Use of Fonts 10 credit
Total 100 credit

Then the total credit after addition will be converted on a scale of 10 and then evaluated.
