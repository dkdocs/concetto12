Greenism

Because things are the way they are, things will not stay the way they were.
So, Lets, Dream of a cleaner and greener ISM.
Objective: Ideas to make the campus green and ecofriendly.
Area under observation: ISM campus.
Ideas: Different kind of ideas can be implemented in this regard some of which are:

  • Increasing Greenery.
  • Mitigating waste.
  • Regulating and checking wastewater.
  • Rest use your extraordinary brains.

Event

  • Team of 2-3 members.
  • Submit self explanatory ideas.
  • Presentation of your ideas.

It consists of two rounds.

1st round

  • Submission of ideas on paper and to give presentation.
  • Top 5 groups will move to the 2nd round.

2nd round

  • To plan your Green city on paper.
  • Best green city will be awarded.
