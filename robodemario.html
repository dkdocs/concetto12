ROBO-de MARIO

Introduction

In the last many years since its inception, Techfest has stayed true to its motto of spreading the best of science and technology among the masses. And providing a fair playing field for anyone in India to prove their mettle at the forefront of technical supremacy has been among its priorities. With the advent of new technologies by the day, robots would soon live in harmony with human-kind.

Problem Statement

To design and create a manual robot imagining your bot to be Mario which can come over various hurdles. BOT's aim is to complete each stage in minimum time interval. BOT should be fully capable of maneuvering in a dust and rugged terrain. Depending on best performance of each robot they will qualify for subsequent rounds.

"ROBOTICS IS ABOUT US.IT IS THE DISCIPLINE OF EMULATING OUR LIVES, ABOUT WONDERING HOW IT WORKS".

Bot Specification

  • Dimension of bot must not exceed 30*30*30cm which entering the start zone.
  • All mechanism and other accessories can be used only by the bot.
  • The bot must not split up into any fractional parts during the play.
  • Bots cannot be constructed using readymade kits or any readymade mechanism. But one can make use of gear mechanism by organizers.
  • The bot must use on board power supply only.
  • The team must come prepared with wires around 4m long.
  • The bot should be compact in order to efficiently tackle the arena.

Game Play

  • The bot should be starting from their respective starting zone mentioned in the arena.
  • There will be three rounds.
  • This competition is to be matched on the basic concept of Mario game.
  • Different mechanism would be checked like hooking, pushing, pulling, dumping, carrying etc.
  • ROBO-de MARIO consists of three different rounds:
    1. La creation de MARIO : In this level there will be two paths on which blocks will be placed .The bot has to pick all the blocks by overcoming the hurdles and has to arrange them such that it forms an image of Mario. Path can be chosen as per participants choice, and once chose can't change the track.
    2. Robo Crate: In this level the bot has to pass through hurdles and can get an advantage of time by blasting the balloons within minimum interval of time. In this level mechanism of hooking, pulling, and pinning would be tested. Bots with minimum completing time of the event will eventually have a positive side.
    3. Sauver La Princesses: In this level the bot has to pass the hurdles to finally save the princess through higher level of difficulties. Wishing the best Mario robot to reach the destined goal.

General Instruction

  1. Each team can consist of a maximum of 5 members. The team members can be from different institutes or colleges.
  2. No two teams can have a common member.
  3. Only a single robot must be used by the team.
  4. Any team exceeding the specified dimensions of the robot must make necessary changes to satisfy the given dimensions, else they'll be disqualified.
  5. Teams must adhere to the spirit of healthy competition. Intentionally damaging any other team's robot will not be tolerated under any circumstances.
  6. No robot must damage the arena. Punishments ranging from penalties to disqualification can be imposed.
  7. The Organizers reserve the right to change any of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any, will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered participants.
  8. Any team that is not ready at the time specified shall be disqualified.
  9. The decision of the organizing team will be final and binding.
  11. Some specific rules will be given for each event, sustaining the excitement about the adaption keeping the roadie spirit alive.

Scoring

Level 1:

Points are completely based on the time taken to complete the task. Task has to be completed in minimum time. In case of tie one reaching the checkpoint first will get advantage.

Level 2:

Max Time:

  1. Manual handling: +15sec.
  2. Hurdle Crossing : +30sec.
  3. Balloon Bursting : -5sec.
