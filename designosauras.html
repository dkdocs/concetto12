Introduction

Tired of being a passive onlooker while other built masterpieces..?? Here's a chance to put your innovative ideas to practice, an event that will test your creativity and passion for engineering. Be ready to compete with best minds of the technical world.

Prelims

Include building a bridge that could sustain maximum load & minimum deflection.This will test your modelling skills & implement the best design into reality.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Maximum 4 members are allowed in a team.
  2. All the required materials will be provided on the spot participants are not allowed to bring any material from their side.
  3. Newspapers should not be fed to the cigarette boxes.
  4. The time given to the participants will be 1.5 hours.
  5. The top 5 teams will qualify for the advanced round.

Materials

Boxes, match sticks, thread, office pins, straws, glue, newspaper, ice-cream sticks, cutter *only limited material will be provided for the construction of bridge Physical Specifications of the bridge are:

  • Height of deck ≥ 10 centimeter
  • Span of bridge ≥ 50 centimeter
  • Pier spacing ≥ 10 centimeter
  • Width of deck ≥ 15 centimeter

Testing

  • This round will check the dimensional specifications of individual bridges regarding height of the deck, pier spacing, weight.
  • Dead loads will be applied and gradually increased on the weakest part of the bridge span until failure occurs. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T2ZyqNODJpU

Advanced

And in the advanced round you have to create hurdles that would delay a ball for the maximum time while rolling down a slope which would be primarily made of cardboard or thermocol.

Rules And Regualations

  1. Identical cardboard bases will be provided to all the teams.
  2. All the required materials will be provided on the spot.
  3. Dimensions and inclination of the board will be revealed on the spot.
  4. f the ball stucks on its way, then the time penalty will be "2n!" seconds, where n is the number of times the ball gets stucked.
  5. The time given to the participants will be 3 hours.
  6. ufficient time will be given for the POP and other materials to dry.

Materials

Thermocol, Cardboard , POP (Plaster of Paris), clay , wood, Water, Cutter, Glue and other materials (to create hurdles).
