Ecologia

Introduction

Ecologia is a case study-cum-presentation event, an unparalleled event enveloping the wide diversities of mineral, mining, geology, energy & environmental sciences, an event to bring out the engineer's erudition. A case study based on real time problems provided and judged by eminent professionals.

Eligibility

  • Participants must be pursuing under graduate or post graduate programme in recognized college.
  • Participants should have original ID card provided by their respective college during the competition.

Rules and Regulations

  • Abstract should be precise and to the topic.
  • Solution to the case must be practically possible.
  • Abstract should be submitted within 10 days of the release of topic

Second runner up Rs. 1000/-
Participants will be provided Certificates.
