Hospitality

The Indian School of Mines (ISM) is staging yet another edition of Concetto – the Annual Techfest from 4th to 6th of October 2012. It provides a platform for the most brilliant minds across the country to exhibit their talents and competitive skills. One of the major concerns in the organization of such a mega event rests on hospitality.

Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad has been long known for its vibrant culture, tradition and hospitality. The hospitality team of the event intends to carry this legacy of ISM forward by maintaining a healthy host-guest relationship. It will be the utmost pleasure of the hospitality team to make all its guest feel at home through highly warm and affectionate service.

Participants from all across India are thus cordially invited to be a part of this mega techno- management event. It is our humble request to the interested candidates to register themselves or atleast inform our team beforehand about the exact date and time of their arrival in order to avoid any last minute rush and inconvenience. FAQ

Where is Indian School Of Mines?

I.S.M. is situated in the city of Dhanbad in the state of Jharkhand.It is at a distance of 4km from Dhanbad Railway Station.

How do I reach Dhanbad?

Dhanbad is situated around 270 kms from Kolkata.All important trains including Rajdhani & Shatabdi Express stop here. If you are coming from North, take either Rajdhani Express, Poorva Express, Kalka Mail, Jammu-Tawi Express, or New-Delhi-Howrah Express. In addition Purushottam Express and Jharkhand Express pass through Gomoh, which is 30 minutes from Dhanbad. If you are coming from Bombay ,Bombay Howrah mail is the direct train to Dhanbad. There is one bi-weekly train from Ahmedabad to Dhanbad. From South –Allepy Dhanbad Express is the direct train. Otherwise come to Howrah and from Howrah you have a large number of trains to Dhanbad. The nearest airport connected to all major cities of the country is Kolkata Airport. More details about jharkhand state can be seen on websites:
www.jharkhand.nic.in
www.jharkhandgov.com

How do i reach Indian School of mines,after reaching Dhanbad Railway station?

Indian School of mines is 4 km away from dhanbad railway station and can be reached easily through auto-rickshaw and taxi from the railway station,though CONCETTO's hospitality team would be taking care of you after you reach the Dhanbad railway station.

What do I do after reaching ISM?

Participants are required to report and register themselves at the registration desk of CONCETTO'12 ,at the earliest.

what would be the schedule of events being organised?

The schedule of events would be posted on the website www.concetto.co.in and would be available on the registration desk too.

What about the Eating facilities?

Hospitality Team would be providing you with food in the dining block of ISM hostels.A variety of food stalls shall be set up in the festive area which will cater to your food requirements.Apart from that there are other eating outlets like subway ,café cofee day ,etc. in and around the campus.

Where would I be accomodated?

Accomodation to outstation participants would be provided by hospitality team of concetto inside ISM campus.

What should I bring with me?

It is mandatory for every participant to bring their respective ID cards.

What about the hospital facilities?

Indian School of Mines has its own health Centre;in case you fall ill or are dealt with an accident you should immediately report it to the registration desk.You would be treated immediately.

What about Security facilities?

Indian School Of Mines has a vigilnt and round the clock security service.To ensure safety of participants during the fest,there will be additonal security guards in the hostels and ISM premises.

What about certificates and prize money?

The prize money and the prize would be given to winning participants from prize and prize money desk.The prize money is event specific,and can be found out from the website.

Is there any ATM inside the campus?

Yes,there is one SBI ATM inside the campus and no. of other banks' ATM around the campus.

Will there be any kind of transpotational facility from the railway station to the ISM campus?

Yes, we would be running buses several times a day and hospitality team would be there to receive you.
