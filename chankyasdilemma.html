Chankya's Dilemma

Introduction

Concetto 2k12 presents Case study, a chance to showcase your managerial skills! Case study, one of its kind to inspire young minds into realizing their dreams. Just managerial skills will not suffice to take you through though; only technically viable analysis will get the job done here. Analyze problems from different viewpoints and unleash your analytical skills. Giving a management perspective to the technical festival, this event is all set to be the highlight of concetto 2k12!! Form teams of three and get ready to participate in an enthralling competition to find the potential in you. Are you the chosen one?

General

  • This event will be held in 3 stages.
  • The first stage will be online while the second and third will be held during Concetto at ISM Dhanbad.
  • Case will be uploaded soon.
  • Send your solution with subject "CASE STUDY" to genesis@ismu.ac.in by the last date of submission.
  • A maximum of three participants per team are allowed.

Stage 1

  • In this stage the teams are required to submit a comprehensive solution to the case. They have to submit the solution in a PDF format or word document format.
  • All submissions must be done before 25th September, 12:00 P.M.
  • Short listing will be done on the basis of this round.
  • 10 teams will qualify for next stage.

Stage 2

  • This is a presentation round.
  • Teams will discuss their solutions exhaustively and present the salient features to the judges and other teams giving justifications wherever necessary.
  • The presentation time for second round is 10 minutes and 5 minutes for questions by judges and other teams.
  • 5 Teams are shortlisted for the final round.

Stage 3

  • This is the final round, 5 teams will qualify for this round.
  • Teams will be facing 'Dynamically Changing' situations, in the form of questions to be asked by panel.
  • This is followed a panel discussion and the most feasible solution is adjudged the winner followed by first runner-up and second runner-up.
